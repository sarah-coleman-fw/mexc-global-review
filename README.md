# MEXC Global Review: What a Financial Counselor Wants You to Know
*By Sarah Coleman, MA Financial Counseling (Fordham) | Financial Wellness Counselor*

In my decade of financial counseling work, I've seen countless clients navigate the turbulent waters of cryptocurrency investment. This **MEXC Global review** comes from a financial wellness perspective — designed to help you make an informed decision before committing your money.

## What Is MEXC Global?
MEXC Global is a cryptocurrency exchange headquartered in Seychelles, founded in 2018. It offers trading in over 1,500 cryptocurrencies and has grown to serve millions of users worldwide.

## Fee Structure Analysis
MEXC Global boasts zero maker fees on spot trading, which is genuinely attractive from a cost perspective. Withdrawal fees vary by asset but are generally competitive. For clients I counsel who are exploring crypto as part of a diversified portfolio, fees matter enormously over time — and MEXC performs well here.

## Risk Assessment
No honest **MEXC Global review** can ignore the risks. Cryptocurrency trading carries extreme volatility. MEXC's futures products with up to 200x leverage are instruments that can eliminate capital rapidly.

## Accessibility and Support
The platform offers 24/7 customer support and a reasonably intuitive interface. Mobile and desktop apps are stable and functional. However, US residents face access restrictions due to regulatory limitations.

## Counselor's Verdict
A legitimate, feature-rich exchange with competitive pricing, but one that requires serious financial discipline. Build your emergency fund first. Never invest borrowed money.
